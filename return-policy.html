Returns & Refund Policy

Effective date: 31 Aug 2025

At AHM Studios, our goal is to deliver exceptional creative work. This policy explains when returns or refunds may apply and how to request them.

1. Scope

  • Services (creative work): Due to the nature of bespoke services, refunds are generally not offered once work has commenced. We provide revisions per your package to address reasonable concerns.
  • Digital deliverables: Non-tangible goods (e.g., downloadable/online files) are non-returnable once delivered/accessed, except where required by law or where files are defective.
  • Physical goods (if any): Eligible for return within 14 days of delivery if unused, in original condition/packaging.

  1. Email [YOUR EMAIL] with your order number, item/service, reason, and any supporting details (e.g., screenshots, photos).
  2. We’ll acknowledge within 2–3 business days and advise next steps.
  3. For physical goods, you are responsible for safe return shipping to: [POSTAL ADDRESS], Colombo, Sri Lanka. Keep proof of postage.

3. Refund method & timelines

  • Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method where possible; otherwise via bank transfer in LKR.
  • Allow 7–14 business days for processing after approval; card issuers may take additional time to reflect the credit.

4. Non-returnable / non-refundable

  • Customized services once delivered or substantially performed.
  • Digital files already delivered/accessed (unless defective).
  • Change-of-mind outside the stated windows.

5. Defects & issues
